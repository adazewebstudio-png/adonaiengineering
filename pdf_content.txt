2. About Us
Company Overview
Adonai   Engineering   and   Construction   Ltd   is   a   professional   engineering   and   construction   company
delivering   disciplined,   high-quality   construction   solutions   across   buildings,   estate   developments,   and
supporting infrastructure.
Established   in   2021,   the   company   has   executed   construction   works   directly   across   residential   and
commercial developments and is now formally positioned as a market-facing construction firm serving both
group and third-party clients.
Group Context
Adonai Engineering and Construction Ltd is a subsidiary of Adonai Estate Limited. The company has gained
extensive execution experience delivering construction works within a broader real estate ecosystem and
operates independently as a construction partner to external clients.
Mission
To   deliver   reliable,   high-quality   construction   solutions   through   disciplined   execution,   strong   project
controls, and technical integrity.
Vision
To   grow   into   a   respected   African   construction   brand   known   for   dependable   execution   and   enduring
structures.

3. Services
Building Construction
We   deliver   building   construction   services   across   residential,   commercial,   hospitality,   and   institutional
developments.   Projects   are   executed   directly   through   experienced   site   teams   supported   by   structured
project controls.
Estate & Property Development Construction
We   provide   turnkey   and   phased   construction   services   for   estate   and   property   developments,   including
internal infrastructure and bulk works.
Civil Works & Development Infrastructure
We undertake civil works associated with urban growth and property development, including estate roads,
access   roads,   drainage   systems,   and   earthworks.   Delivery   is   aligned   to   project   scale   and   technical
requirements.
Project Execution & Construction Management
We provide construction planning, cost and quality control, site supervision, subcontractor coordination,
and compliance oversight to ensure disciplined delivery.
