# Tema Industrial Complex

This flagship project demonstrates our capability in large-scale industrial construction. The facility includes:

- 40,000 sqm of warehouse space.
- 5,000 sqm of cold storage.
- Administrative offices and staff quarters.

## Civil Works

The project required extensive civil works, including:
- Soil stabilization for marshy terrain.
- 5km of internal road networks.
- A dedicated drainage system connected to the municipal grid.

## Outcome

Delivered 2 weeks ahead of schedule, this facility now handles 20% of the client's regional logistics.
